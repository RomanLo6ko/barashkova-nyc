Training

Training with Evgenia Barashkova

Private, one-to-one instruction in draping, garment construction and fit — for designers, dressmakers and dedicated beginners.

The Method

Learn the craft from a maker, not a manual

This is not a shortcut or a weekend course. It is a serious apprenticeship in method — how a garment is really built — taught individually and at the level you are ready for.

  • Draping on the form — translating an idea into cloth
  • Pattern-making and pattern development
  • Understanding fabric, grain and behaviour
  • Fit, posture and proportion
  • Structured interiors and couture-level construction
  • From sketch to finished, wearable piece
  • Working methodically and to a standard
  • Individual guidance for your own projects
Who it's for

For beginners and working professionals

Designers

Learn to bring your own sketches to life with correct construction, fit and finishing.

Dressmakers

Deepen your draping and construction technique and raise your work to couture level.

Beginners

Build real foundations — from the form and the pattern to a finished, well-made garment.
