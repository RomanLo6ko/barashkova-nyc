About

Evgenia Barashkova

A New York-based Head Draper and garment construction specialist.

Evgenia transforms a designer's vision into beautifully constructed garments with precision, structure and attention to every detail. Her work is grounded in classical pattern-making, draping and the discipline of couture-level construction.

With experience in complex costume and dress construction for New York's theatrical and entertainment industry, she brings a rigor rarely found in everyday dressmaking to private, made-to-measure work.
